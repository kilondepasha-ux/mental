About YMHC

Our mission is to enable every young person to access timely mental health support—without stigma or barriers.

Our Story

Founded by students and young professionals, YMHC emerged from a simple insight: early conversations save lives. We blend peer leadership, practitioner guidance, and technology to deliver practical support.

Goals

  • Increase help-seeking behavior by 30% in partner schools.
  • Train 100+ peer supporters annually.
  • Expand an open resource hub for youth and caregivers.
